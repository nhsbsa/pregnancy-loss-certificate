We cannot provide a baby loss certificate

You can only use this service if you live in England.

If you live in Scotland, you can apply for an entry in the Memorial Book. This is a commemorative record held by National Records of Scotland.

Once the loss has been recorded in the Memorial Book, a commemorative certificate will be sent to the applicant free of charge.

Support organisations

  • NHS How to access mental health services
  • Antenatal Results and Choices (ARC)
  • Daddy’s with Angels
  • Petals
  • Sands
  • Saying Goodbye
  • The Ectopic Pregnancy Trust
  • The Miscarriage Association
  • Tommy's
