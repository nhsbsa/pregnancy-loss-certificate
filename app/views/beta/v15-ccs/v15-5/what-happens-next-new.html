What happens next

We’ll ask you for the other parent’s date of birth. This is because we need to match their details with their GP record, if they give permission for their name to be on the certificate.

If the date of birth you enter for the other parent does not match the date they enter, your request for a certificate will be cancelled.

Your request may take up to 21 days if you add the other parent's name to the certificate. This is because the other parent will have up to 7 days to give permission before the request is complete.

If we get their permission, you'll then receive the certificate within 14 days.

If the other parent chooses not to be named on the certificate, your request will be cancelled.

You'll be able to start again and request a certificate that shows only your name as a parent of your baby.
